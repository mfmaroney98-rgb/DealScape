Point to clarify, none of these NEED to be filled out, since it’s basically just a filter.
Information the Sell-Side Uploads:
·       Financial Information (can the user input X number years based on their choice?)
o   Revenue
o   Gross Profit
o   Gross Margin (can be calculated or entered manually by user, just Gross Profit / Revenue)
o   EBITDA
o   EBITDA Margin (EBITDA / Revenue)
o   EBIT
o   EBIT Margin (EBIT / Revenue)
o   Net Income
o   Net Margin (Net Income / Revenue)
o   Could throw a CAGR or Year-over-Year Growth Calculation in too 
·       Company Information
o   Company Name
o   Anonymized Company Name or Title (for instance: “Leading Material Handling Equipment Manufacturers”)
o   Project Name (if applicable)
o   Location (City, State, Region, Country, Continent, maybe even the address, it’s the seller’s discretion)
o   Number of employees
o   Keywords
o   Industry / NAICS Codes
o   Company type (LLC, S-Corp, etc.)
o   Company Ownership (Private Company, Private Equity-owned, Corporate Subsidiary, Public Company, etc.)
·       Transaction Type
o   Transaction Type (Total Sale, Acquisition of Majority Stake, Debt Raise, Minority Equity Raise, Mezzanine Financing, Divestiture / Carve-out, etc.)
o  For the Minority Equity, we can include funding rounds like Angel, Seed, Series A, B, C, etc.)
·       Other
o   Founder-owned Company (check box)
o   Female-owned Company (check box)
o   Minority-owned Company (check box)
o   Family-owned Company (check box)
 
Information the Buy-Side Enters for the search:
·       Financial Information
o   Preferred Revenue Range (Min and Max, does not need to be both)
o   Same for all the other financial metrics. Point being these will be ranges as opposed to specific values
o   Could throw a CAGR or Year-over-Year Growth Calculation in too
·       Company Information
o   Location (some buyers only buy in the Midwest, or Ohio, etc.)
o   Number of Employees (Min and Max, does not need to be both)
o   Keywords
o   Industry / NAICS Codes
o   Company type (LLC, S-Corp, etc.)
o   Company Ownership (Private Company, Private Equity-owned, Corporate Subsidiary, Public Company, etc.)
·       Transaction Type
o   Transaction Type (Total Sale, Acquisition of Majority Stake, Debt Raise, Minority Equity Raise, Mezzanine Financing, Divestiture / Carve-out, etc.)
o  For the Minority Equity, we can include funding rounds like Angel, Seed, Series A, B, C, etc.)
·       Other
o   Founder-owned Company (check box)
o   Female-owned Company (check box)
o   Minority-owned Company (check box)
o   Family-owned Company (check box)
